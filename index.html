About the Project

Are you curious about how we can make the next generation of digital applications safer for everyone? During my internship at The Hashgraph Group, I developed a powerful tool that helps protect decentralized apps and smart contracts from cyber threats.

This tool combines smart computer analysis, expert review, and real-time monitoring to give developers and organizations peace of mind—before and after their apps go live. In just a few minutes, it checks for hidden risks and provides clear, easy-to-follow advice for fixing any issues.

Smarter Scans: Finds security problems that others might miss, using both advanced technology and human expertise.
Live Protection: Watches over apps after they're launched and sends instant alerts if something suspicious happens.
Fast & Reliable: Delivers results quickly and accurately, helping teams fix problems before they become serious.

By making security easier and more effective, this project helps everyone—from tech experts to everyday users—trust the apps they use.

Want to know more? Join my graduate defense to see how this innovation is making the digital world safer for all!
